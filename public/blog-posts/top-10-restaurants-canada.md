Canada's restaurant scene blends indigenous ingredients with global influences, creating unique dining experiences. Here are the top 10 restaurants that represent the country's culinary diversity and excellence.

## 1. Hawksworth Restaurant (Vancouver) - Contemporary Canadian
Chef David Hawksworth's flagship offers Pacific Northwest ingredients with European techniques. The tasting menu is exceptional.

## 2. Toqué! (Montreal) - French-Inspired Quebecois
Innovative Quebec cuisine with French foundations. The wine pairing experience is legendary.

## 3. Deer + Almond (Vancouver) - Plant-Based Innovation
Zero-waste, plant-based dining that's creative and delicious. A pioneer in sustainable fine dining.

## 4. St. Lawrence (Vancouver) - Market-Driven Excellence
Focuses on local, seasonal ingredients with global influences. The brunch is particularly noteworthy.

## 5. Maison Publique (Montreal) - Casual French Bistro
Authentic French bistro fare in a relaxed setting. The steak frites and moules-frites are perfect.

## 6. Quails' Gate Estate Wines (Kelowna) - Winery Dining
Pairing Okanagan wines with Mediterranean-inspired cuisine in a stunning vineyard setting.

## 7. The Keg Steakhouse + Bar (Multiple Locations) - Classic Steakhouse
Canada's premier steakhouse chain with consistent quality and an extensive wine list.

## 8. Hy's Steakhouse (Toronto) - Toronto Institution
Classic steakhouse with a club-like atmosphere. The prime rib and martinis are legendary.

## 9. Rodney's Oyster House (Toronto) - Seafood Specialist
Fresh oysters and seafood in a historic setting. The raw bar is exceptional.

## 10. Clafoutis (Ottawa) - French Bakery-Cafe
Authentic French pastries and light meals. A neighborhood gem for casual dining.
