Eating a healthy lunch can have a big impact on your energy levels and overall well-being. Understanding the benefits of a good lunch break is the first step toward a more productive day. Here are five tips for making healthier choices with the help of Lunch Hub.

## Simple Swaps for a Better Meal

1.  **Choose Lean Proteins:** Look for grilled chicken, fish, or plant-based options like tofu and beans.
2.  **Load Up on Veggies:** Salads, steamed vegetables, and veggie-packed soups are all great choices.
3.  **Opt for Whole Grains:** Brown rice, quinoa, and whole-wheat bread provide sustained energy.
4.  **Watch Out for Dressings and Sauces:** Ask for them on the side to control how much you use.
5.  **Stay Hydrated:** Drink plenty of water before, during, and after your meal.
