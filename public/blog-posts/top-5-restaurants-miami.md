Miami's food scene is as diverse and colorful as the city itself. Known for its Cuban influences and fresh Atlantic seafood, these top 5 restaurants are worth a visit.

## 1. Joe's Stone Crab - A Miami Institution
For over 100 years, Joe's has been serving up the world's most famous stone crabs. It's a classic Miami experience, perfect for a special occasion or a taste of true Magic City history.

## 2. Versailles - The Heart of Little Havana
Versailles is more than just a restaurant; it's a cultural center in the heart of Little Havana. Famous for its Cuban sandwiches and cafecitos, this is the place to experience authentic Cuban culture.

## 3. KYU - Modern Wood-Fired Asian
In the trendy Wynwood neighborhood, KYU offers a unique blend of wood-fired Asian-inspired dishes. The smoked beef short rib and roasted cauliflower are fan favorites.

## 4. Stubborn Seed - Michellin-Starred Creativity
Chef Jeremy Ford's Stubborn Seed in South Beach is a playground for culinary innovation. The tasting menu showcases seasonal ingredients in surprising and beautiful ways.

## 5. Lucali - Brick-Oven Pizza Masterpiece
While originally from Brooklyn, Lucali Miami has quickly become a local favorite. With its minimalist menu and incredible brick-oven pizzas, it's a must-visit for pizza lovers.
