Toronto's diverse food scene offers something for every palate. From upscale dining to hidden gems, here are the top 5 restaurants that consistently impress locals and visitors alike.

## 1. Canoe - Fine Dining with a View
Perched atop the TD Bank Tower, Canoe serves contemporary Canadian cuisine with panoramic city views. The tasting menu featuring local ingredients is a highlight.

## 2. Richmonde - Classic French Elegance
A Toronto institution since 1978, Richmonde offers impeccable French service and dishes like duck confit and escargot. The wine list is extensive and expertly curated.

## 3. Aloette - Creative French Bistro
Chef Patrick Kriss's innovative takes on French classics in a cozy Ossington Avenue setting. Don't miss the duck rillettes and seasonal specials.

## 4. Buca - Italian Excellence
Authentic Italian cuisine in a lively atmosphere. The ossobuco and fresh pastas are standout dishes, perfect for groups or special occasions.

## 5. Khao San Road - Thai Street Food
Authentic Thai flavors in a casual setting. The pad thai and green curry capture the essence of Bangkok street food right in Toronto.
